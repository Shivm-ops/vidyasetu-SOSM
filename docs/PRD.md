# VIDYASETU — Product Requirement Document

**Version:** 1.0  
**Platform:** India's most comprehensive AI-powered, Marathi-first educational ecosystem  
**Tagline:** Bridging Knowledge, Skills, Career and Community  

---

## 1. Executive Summary

VidyaSetu is a complete educational ecosystem for students Class 1–12 in rural and semi-urban Maharashtra. It goes beyond academics to address the complete development of a student: education, life skills, digital literacy, financial literacy, career readiness, communication, entrepreneurship, community participation, innovation, leadership, and wellbeing.

## 2. Problem Statement

Rural Maharashtra students face:
- **Language barrier**: Most EdTech is English-first
- **Connectivity gap**: Unreliable or no internet in villages
- **Device limitation**: Budget Android phones, shared family devices
- **Ecosystem gap**: Academic apps only, no life skills / career guidance
- **Teacher burnout**: Manual attendance, homework, reports leave no time for teaching
- **Parent disconnect**: Parents can't read English progress reports
- **Career blindness**: Students don't know careers beyond engineering/medicine/government jobs

## 3. Target Users

| User | Primary Device | Language | Key Need |

## 4. Core Features by Stage

### Foundation Stage (Class 1-4)
- Alphabet learning (Marathi + English + Numbers)
- AI Story Teller (Marathi stories with moral values)
- Voice-guided navigation (for pre-literate users)
- Picture-based UI
- Parent-assisted learning mode
- Memory games, drawing activities

### Discovery Stage (Class 5-8)
- Maharashtra State Board curriculum (all subjects)
- Interactive lessons with videos
- AI Homework Helper
- Science experiments, math activities
- Coding Basics (Scratch-like)
- Digital Literacy module
- Financial Literacy basics
- Communication Skills

### Career Stage (Class 9-12)
- Board exam preparation (SSC/HSC)
- Scholarship discovery (100+ scholarships)
- Career Explorer (50+ career paths in Marathi)
- Competitive exam guidance (NEET, JEE, MPSC, etc.)
- Resume builder
- Interview preparation
- AI Career Counselor

## 5. Teacher Platform Features

- **Attendance**: One-tap daily attendance with absent SMS to parents
- **AI Lesson Planner**: Generate lesson plans in Marathi in 30 seconds
- **Question Paper Generator**: AI-generated papers aligned to syllabus
- **Homework**: Assign and track submissions
- **Analytics Heatmap**: Identify struggling students early
- **Parent Communication**: Broadcast messages in Marathi

## 6. Parent Platform Features (Aai-Baba Mode)

- Weekly progress report in simple Marathi
- Attendance alerts (SMS + push notification)
- Exam schedule and result notifications
- Child's mood trends
- Scholarship application status
- Direct message to teacher

## 7. Unique Innovations

1. **Wari Learning Journey** — Gamification inspired by the annual Wari pilgrimage; students earn points/badges on their educational journey
2. **Aai-Baba Voice Mode** — Parents receive voice calls summarizing child's week in Marathi
3. **Gram Innovation Lab** — Community projects where students solve local village problems
4. **Digital Skill Passport** — Verifiable record of every skill/certificate a student earns
5. **Village Learning Circles** — Peer groups at village level, not just school level
6. **Offline School Hub** — Raspberry Pi device serves content to entire school without internet
7. **Localized Career Discovery** — Career paths relevant to Maharashtra villages (e.g., floriculture, agri-tech, cooperative sector)
8. **Marathi AI Teacher** — First EdTech AI tutor that thinks, speaks, and teaches in Marathi
